About Tswanebusmate

Tswanebusmate is a revolutionary bus tracking and transport management platform designed to make public transport more efficient, reliable, and accessible. With real-time tracking, schedule viewing, credit purchases, AI-powered alerts, and alternative transport suggestions, our goal is to enhance commuter convenience and safety in Tshwane.

Our platform leverages cutting-edge technology to provide accurate, up-to-the-minute bus location data and timely alerts about delays or disruptions. We believe that informed commuters are happier commuters, and Tswanebusmate is here to make that a reality.

Our Mission

To modernize public transportation in Tshwane by integrating smart solutions that improve efficiency, safety, and user satisfaction. We aim to create a seamless commuting experience that encourages more people to choose public transport.

Our Vision

To be the leading public transport management platform in South Africa, providing innovative solutions that connect people and places seamlessly. We envision a future where public transport is the preferred choice for all Tshwane residents.
